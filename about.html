"The Light in the Darkness." The story inspired by a million of thoughts encountered on the roadsides (also from the famous composition named "The little match girl") during the exploration of Durga puja pandals there lies a little soul trying to find light even when she has a very little space of light being completely surrounded by darkness , the story reflects the idea of festives are not as bright as for everyone the freedom we get to experiance every inch of essence of light, charishma and happiness of being with our loved ones not meant for everyone even after they deserves the same. i would also request every reader who is reading it please have a act of gratitude and respect for those who are there to serve us the things that makes our festive complete...
